## HOW TO SEND

1. **Personalize the intro** — Mention something specific to this investor
2. **Lead with the strongest metric** — Usually WAD or MRR growth
3. **Keep it under 2 pages** — Investors are busy
4. **Include one specific ask** — Don't be shy
5. **Follow up in 1 week** — If no response, send a brief nudge
