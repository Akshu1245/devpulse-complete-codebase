## Recording Tips

1. **Setup:**
   - Browser: Chrome, zoom 100%, no bookmarks bar
   - Theme: Dark mode (matches RakshEx UI)
   - Resolution: 1920x1080
   - Mouse: Highlight cursor (Loom setting)

2. **Voiceover:**
   - Speak slowly (120 WPM)
   - Pause 1 second between scenes
   - Use a quiet room or noise-cancelling mic

3. **Editing:**
   - Add text overlays in post (Canva or Descript)
   - No music needed — voiceover only
   - Total length: 58-62 seconds

4. **Hosting:**
   - Upload to YouTube (unlisted) for investor sharing
   - Embed on landing page hero
   - Add to Product Hunt launch post
