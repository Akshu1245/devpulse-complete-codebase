## Best Practices

- **Personalize the first line** based on their company news, role, or recent post
- **Keep under 120 words** — busy people skim
- **One ask only** — demo, reply, or click
- **Track opens** with a lightweight tool (not required, but helpful)
- **Follow up 3 times** with 4-day gaps before moving on
