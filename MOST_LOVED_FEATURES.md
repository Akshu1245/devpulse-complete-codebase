## RANKING METHODOLOGY

Features are scored on:

1. **Frequency** — How often users mention it unprompted
2. **Emotion** — How strongly they feel (exclamation marks, superlatives)
3. **Retention correlation** — Do users who love it stay longer?
4. **Referral correlation** — Do they tell others about it?

**Love Score = (Frequency × 0.3) + (Emotion × 0.3) + (Retention × 0.25) + (Referral × 0.15)**
